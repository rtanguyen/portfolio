A bit about me

Hello World! I'm Rebecca Nguyen, a Full Stack Web Developer living in Austin, Texas.

My interest in web development started back in highschool when I wanted to try creating custom Tumblr themes (honorable mention to Xanga). After college, I started my career at Deloitte, staying for a few years before deciding to enroll in the Coding Boot Camp at UT Austin.

A former accountant with a creative streak, I love combining logic and design to create eye-catching and innovative user experiences.

In my spare time, you can find me cooking, playing with my pup Chuy, or getting lost in a fantasy novel.
